Your upcoming phases

Menstrual Phase 🩸

Best for

Rest, recovery, and lower-key plans.

  • Cancel or keep light anything demanding.
  • Do admin cleanup, journaling, and reflection.
  • Gentle walks, yoga, and quiet work.
  • Avoid stacking lots of social events or intense workouts.
Follicular Phase 🌱

Best for

Starting things and building momentum.

  • Schedule brainstorming, planning, learning, and new projects.
  • Put workouts, creative work, and "first draft" tasks here.
  • Good time for networking, meetings, and trying new habits.
Ovulation Phase ✨

Best for

Visibility, connection, and high energy.

  • Put presentations, interviews, dates, and important meetings here.
  • Great for collaborative work, launches, content recording, and big social plans.
  • Also a good window for harder workouts.
Luteal Phase 🌙

Best for

Finishing, refining, and winding down.

  • Schedule editing, reviewing, budgeting, organizing, and wrapping up projects.
  • Keep your calendar a bit lighter as energy may dip.
  • Favor calmer social plans, routines, and lower-intensity movement.
Luteal – Lower Energy 🌫️

Best for

Being gentle with yourself.

  • Energy is dipping — rest, reduce commitments, and prioritise self-care.
  • Avoid scheduling anything high-stakes or draining.
  • Simple routines and quiet time go a long way.

Simple Calendar Rule

Menstrual = Rest
Follicular = Create
Ovulation = Connect
Luteal = Finish

Example: Planning a Launch

Place the prep work in your follicular phase, the live event in ovulatory, and the follow-up and admin in luteal. Keep your period days for recovery and low-pressure tasks.

Cycle syncing is a helpful planning framework, but it's not one-size-fits-all. Adjust based on your own symptoms, cycle length, and energy patterns.
